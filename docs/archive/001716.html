Churrasco in São Paulo PRIME Pictures

Published Aug 6, 2005

Brazil is famous for its Churrasco — all-you-can-eat barbecued meats served tableside by waitrons bearing skewers of the food and sabers to cut out the portions. I especially recommend the chicken hearts; consume with copious guaraná. (Click to enlarge photos.)
