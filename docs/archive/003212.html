Oh My.

Published Apr 2, 2007

I think I’ve said before that, if you aren’t watching Battlestar Galactica, you’re wasting your television. I would like to revise that statement at this time; if you’re not watching Battlestar Galactica, you’re wasting the entire concept of visual entertainment. Buy it off iTunes. Find a torrent of it. Buy the British version on DVD, probably already out by now. Just see it. I mean, not only is it well-plotted, -written, -directed, and -shot, but now they’ve moved up to showing off — despite having telegraphed everything that was going to be in the season finale, every moment of it left you saying “oh shit!” That, folks, is skill. Don’t be a hata, watch BSG.
