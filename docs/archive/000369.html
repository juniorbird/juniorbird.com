Done!

Published Jan 31, 2004

I’m done! On Thursday, my last application was due (and, yes, I did get it in on time). For the first time since the middle of September, I’m free!

It’s a big relief. It’s nice to have free time every evening and get to work on my little projects. For many people, all of the application work would be replaced by worry over what schools they got into, but it’s not my way to worry over those things. I think I applied to a reasonable selection of schools and should stand a good chance of getting into at least one. So no worries here.

This has been a long and interesting process and I’ve probably learned something from it. But I’m not going to worry about that this weekend. This weekend is all about relaxing! Last night that meant sushi with friends; tonight that means an hour of Cops. And no checking of my online applications!
