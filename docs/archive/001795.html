Mexico City Templo Mayor Pictures

Published Aug 29, 2005

This is it — the very last set of photos from Mexico City. I has a great trip there, and I hope you’ve enjoyed the photos at least 10% as much as I enjoyed taking them. What follows is photos of the ruins of the ancient Aztec Templo Mayor, the center of life in old Tenochtitlan. If you haven’t figured out by now that clicking zooms, then I have no idea what to do with you.
